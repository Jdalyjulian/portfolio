Take a look at what my students are learning

Biosphere Bottles

Self-sustained ecosystems built inside 2 liter bottles.

Case-Based Learning

Case-based learning is a method of learning to elicit what students already know and guide them towards applying this knowledge to solve a puzzle. Along the way, students determine what information is missing and participate in mini-lessons to fill in these gaps.

Plant Mazes

Students test plant stimulus-response scenarios by placing a potted seed inside a box. The box has s hole cut in the top, with obstacles between the pot and the hole. The plant grows towards the light and around the obstacles. A process called phototropism.

Gamify Learning

When it is time to review a concept, students enjoy the game factor. To understand how matter is cycled in an ecosystem, students play trophic level tag, a scientific tag involving the roles of producers, primary consumers, secondary consumers, tertiary consumers, and decomposers.
